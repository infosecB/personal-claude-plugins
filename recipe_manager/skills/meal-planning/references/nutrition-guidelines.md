## Daily Recommended Intake (Adult Average)

- Calories: 2,000
- Protein: 50g
- Carbohydrates: 250-300g
- Fat: 65-80g
- Fiber: 25-30g

## Balanced Meal Plan Principles

- Include protein in every meal
- Mix colors in vegetables for nutrient variety
- Vary protein sources across the week
- Include whole grains
- Limit repeated cuisines on consecutive days
