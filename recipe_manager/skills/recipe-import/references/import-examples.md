# Import Examples

## Example 1: Informal Text

**Input:**
```
My mom's chili recipe - brown a pound of ground beef with onion and garlic, dump in two cans of diced tomatoes, a can of kidney beans (drained), chili powder, cumin, salt. Let it simmer for like 30 min. Serves maybe 6.
```

**Output:**
```markdown
---
title: Mom's Chili
cuisine: American
servings: 6
prep_time: 10 min
cook_time: 30 min
tags: [beef, chili, comfort-food, one-pot]
dietary: [gluten-free]
difficulty: easy
date_added: 2026-03-22
---

## Ingredients
- 1 lb ground beef
- 1 medium onion, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 cans (14.5 oz each) diced tomatoes
- 1 can (15 oz) kidney beans, drained and rinsed
- 2 tbsp chili powder
- 1 tsp cumin
- salt to taste

## Instructions
1. Brown the ground beef in a large pot over medium-high heat, breaking it apart as it cooks. Drain excess fat.
2. Add the onion and garlic. Cook until softened, about 3-4 minutes.
3. Add the diced tomatoes, kidney beans, chili powder, cumin, and salt.
4. Bring to a simmer, reduce heat to low, and cook for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.

## Notes
Family recipe. Adjust chili powder for desired heat level.
```

## Example 2: Bare Ingredient List

**Input:**
```
Pasta aglio e olio
spaghetti, garlic, olive oil, red pepper flakes, parsley
Cook pasta. Fry garlic in oil. Toss together.
```

**Output:**
```markdown
---
title: Pasta Aglio e Olio
cuisine: Italian
servings: 4
prep_time: 5 min
cook_time: 15 min
tags: [pasta, garlic, quick, weeknight, simple]
dietary: [vegan, vegetarian, dairy-free]
difficulty: easy
date_added: 2026-03-22
---

## Ingredients
- 1 lb spaghetti
- 6 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
- 1/3 cup extra-virgin olive oil
- 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
- 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
- salt to taste

## Instructions
1. Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water according to package directions until al dente. Reserve 1 cup pasta water before draining.
2. While pasta cooks, heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-low heat. Add garlic and red pepper flakes. Cook until garlic is golden, about 2-3 minutes.
3. Add drained pasta to the skillet. Toss to coat, adding reserved pasta water a splash at a time to create a light sauce.
4. Remove from heat, add parsley, toss, and serve immediately.

## Notes
Slice garlic thin and cook low and slow so it turns golden without burning.
```
