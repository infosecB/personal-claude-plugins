# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 2026-03-22

## Overview

infosecB's Personal Claude Code Plugins are designed to run entirely on your local machine. Your privacy is straightforward: we don't collect, transmit, or store any of your data.

## Data Collection

These plugins collect **no data whatsoever**. There is no telemetry, no analytics, no tracking, and no external network requests made by the plugins themselves.

## Data Storage

All data created by the plugins (recipes, meal plans, shopping lists) is stored as local files on your machine within your project directory. No data is sent to external servers, databases, or cloud services by the plugins.

## Third-Party Services

These plugins do not integrate with or send data to any third-party services. They operate entirely within the Claude Code or Cowork (Claude Desktop) environment on your device.

Note that Claude Code and Cowork themselves are products of Anthropic and are subject to [Anthropic's Privacy Policy](https://www.anthropic.com/privacy). The plugins run within these tools, so your interactions with Claude are governed by Anthropic's terms, not this policy.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the update will be reflected here and on the [documentation site](https://infosecb.github.io/personal-claude-plugins/privacy.html). Since these plugins have no data collection, meaningful changes are unlikely.
